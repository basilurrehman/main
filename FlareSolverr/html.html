Description:
The Lynwood Unified School District is seeking a qualified general contractor (with A‑ or B‑class California license) to modernize the varsity baseball, softball, and multi‑purpose fields at Lynwood High School. The scope includes complete removal of existing natural turf, extensive site grading, subsurface preparation, irrigation and drainage upgrades, installation of owner‑furnished AstroTurf, construction of a new one‑story team room and storage building, paving, new fencing, entry portal, stadium lighting, scoreboards, dugout shade structures, and accessibility improvements. The contract will be awarded through a formal competitive “Invitation to Bid” process, requires a 10 % bid bond, 100 % performance and payment bonds, compliance with prevailing‑wage rules, a Community Workforce Agreement, and a 3 % DVBE participation goal. • Core disciplines: civil/site work, earthwork, paving, irrigation, synthetic‑turf installation, electrical lighting, fencing, concrete/structural work, and small‑building construction. • Mandatory pre‑bid conference (May 12 2026) and mandatory attendance at the first job walk; a second optional walk on May 27 2026. • Bid submission deadline June 4 2026; construction to start July 13 2026 and run for 355 calendar days, completing July 2 2027.
